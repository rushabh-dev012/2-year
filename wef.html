About Summit Valley University

Our History & Mission

Founded in 1895, Summit Valley University has grown from a small liberal arts college to a comprehensive university offering undergraduate, graduate, and professional degrees. Our historic campus combines classic architecture with state-of-the-art facilities.

Our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that prepares students to think critically, lead ethically, and contribute meaningfully to their professions and communities. We believe in educating the whole person - intellectually, socially, and ethically.
